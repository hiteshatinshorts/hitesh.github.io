About the program

Inshorts Inclusives is a community of like-minded individuals working towards Inshorts' mission of #StayInformed. The Inclusives, being the torchbearers of the mission, are responsible for designing and executing plans and strategies to create awareness of the Inshorts app and promote the habit of news reading amongst the masses. Through this journey, an Inclusive gets exposed to the fields of marketing, operations, business development and strategy and gets a hands on experience in these areas. The Inclusives program also seeks to nurture the creative streak of young minds, by presenting every Inclusive with a fresh plain canvas to paint on, thus allowing them to execute their own ideas.
What's more?
Through these campaigns, the Inclusives are seeking to work towards a much bigger issue that lies in front of us - of keeping everyone informed. Attainment of which has proven to be even more challenging, due to the increasing time crunch and decreasing attention spans. It is this challenge that the Inclusives have taken upon themselves, and have decided to tread on the path of finding innovative solutions to this problem.

The Inshorts App

At Inshorts we aim to provide the simplest way to keep anyone and everyone informed, as we believe that the world would be a better place if people felt more connected to their surroundings and the world at a larger level. We understand that in today’s time, when everyone is busy with their lives, it is difficult to keep a track of things happening around you, that might not be directly impacting you. To overcome this problem, we provide “News in 60 words”, to enable faster news consumption.
